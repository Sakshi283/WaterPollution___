Introduction

  • Water pollution can be defined as alteration in physical,chemical or biological characteristics of water making it unsuitable for designated use in its natural stage.

    • Or,

  • The contamination of water bodies(e.g. lakes,rivers,oceans,aquifers and groundwater) usually caused due to human activities.

  • The substances that cause water pollution are called pollutants.

  • Pollutants include chemicals,trash,bacteria and parasites.

  • It has become a significant problem all across the world.

  • Water is getting polluted day by day due to excessive and careless use, so the percent of available drinking water is reducing.

  • It is very important to understand its causes,effects and controls in order to take effective action to combat it.
